How it works

  1. Scan the barcode (UPC) or QR code on the box. That identifies the exact shoe.
  2. The app fills in the MSRP if it knows the shoe. Type the sticker price, or tap 📷 Auto-fill from a sticker photo to read it with on-device OCR.
  3. You get a discount % vs MSRP — a quick sanity check, not the whole story.
  4. Tap the StockX / GOAT / eBay sold links to see what the shoe actually resells for. That's the real "good deal?" answer.

Everything runs on your phone. Nothing is uploaded. Camera needs an https:// page (GitHub Pages works).
