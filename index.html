How it works

  1. Point the camera so the shoe's label fills the frame — name, size box, and the price sticker.
  2. It reads the model name on the fly (hold steady a second — the Name chip goes green).
  3. Just point the camera at the box — it auto-finds the coloured price sticker (a box appears around it), reads it across many frames, skips blurry ones, and votes out the tag-gun misreads. The preview shows exactly what it sees. Two prices → the lower one is used. Can't get it? Tap the sticker, or type it. Then it saves and you're on to the next box.
  4. Each saved box shows the discount and StockX / GOAT / eBay-sold links — the real "good deal?" answer.

Everything runs on your phone. Nothing is uploaded. Live camera needs an https:// page.
